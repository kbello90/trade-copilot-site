Features

Everything your trade ops team needs.
Nothing they don't.

Built for freight brokers, exporters, importers, and 3PLs who are tired of manual document prep and chasing approvals over email.

4-document suite
CI, PL, CO, and BOL generated from a single Excel upload. Fields populated automatically — no copy-pasting between templates.
AI copilot agent
Type commands in plain English. Approve orders, send documents, check shipment status, list pending approvals — all from one interface.
Document preview
See a full preview of every document before it's sent — header fields, line items, totals, and automatic warnings for missing data.
Automated email delivery
Documents sent via Microsoft Graph with Excel attachments. Worker queue handles retries automatically — no more "did you get my email?"
Shipment tracking
Real-time status board from Draft through Delivered. Every action logged with a full audit trail — know exactly where every shipment stands.
Approval workflow
Multi-step approval with role-based access. Documents can't be sent without proper authorization — compliance built into the workflow.
